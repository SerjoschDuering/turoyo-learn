# Turoyo Language Data for Learning App

> This document serves as the index for all Turoyo language data.
> Due to the volume of data, it is split across multiple files.
> Data is structured for easy conversion to JSON.

## File Index

1. **04a-language-overview.md** - Language classification, writing system, speakers, status
2. **04b-grammar-basics.md** - Sentence structure, gender, verbs, pronouns, tenses
3. **04c-vocabulary-core.md** - Greetings, family members (structured data)
4. **04c2-vocabulary-numbers-colors.md** - Numbers 1-20, higher numbers, colors (structured data)
5. **04d-vocabulary-extended.md** - Food/drinks, body parts, daily verbs (structured data)
6. **04d2-vocabulary-world.md** - Animals, house items, nature, time, adjectives, adverbs (structured data)
7. **04e-phrases-dialogues.md** - Common phrases, dialogues, cultural context, proverbs
8. **04f-exercises.md** - 10 grammar exercises with answers for the app

## Data Accuracy Notes

- Primary sources: Slomo Surayt textbook (Cambridge 2015 standard), Omniglot, academic Swadesh list studies
- Latin transliteration follows the standardized orthography from the International Surayt Conference (Cambridge, August 2015)
- Serto script included where verified; marked [VERIFY] where uncertain
- German translations target the Aramean diaspora community in Germany/Sweden
- Community reference: cheesefrog.github.io/Suryoyo.Simple (comprehensive grammar reference)
